Study Abroad

SOnce the students have sat for their final exam at various local testing centers in Kathmandu, NIEC counsels prospective students for their higher education in foreign countries. To select the right college and university for a Nepali student is very difficult, but well-trained counselors provide all the necessary information about colleges and universities and the lengthy processing system for the student visa application in various embassies.
